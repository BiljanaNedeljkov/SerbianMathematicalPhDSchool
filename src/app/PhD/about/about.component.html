About PhD school

The level of the mathematical sciences in Serbia is rather high and consequently the Doctoral school of mathematics (DSM) is becoming attractive for both domestic and foreign students. DMS provides a well-structured doctoral training in various areas of mathematics. The school relays on joint capacities of four Serbian universities and the Mathematical Institute of the Serbian Academy of Sciences and Arts. This large pool of professors and researchers, together with a number of outstanding visiting professors from various European universities, allows the students to follow their interests under supervision of excellent researchers and to aim at results with significant impact in mathematics. The study program is flexible and offers state-of-the-art knowledge and challenging research topics. PhD programs conducted at DSM aim at having a significant impact at the highest academic levels and boosting sustainable growth.
